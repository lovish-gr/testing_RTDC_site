Our story

Founded on a belief that Rajasthan's artisans deserved a home in the capital.

Mr. Rahul Gupta opened Crafzan — the Rajasthan Textile Development Store — to bring genuine regional craft to South Delhi. Today it stands near Saket as one of the city's most trusted destinations for authentic Rajasthani textile and handicraft.

Every saree, every hand-thrown piece of blue pottery, is sourced directly from the craft clusters of Jaipur, Sanganer, Bagru and Bikaner — depth and provenance that general emporiums rarely match.
